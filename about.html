About

What is REFLECTIONS?

REFLECTIONS is a geometric laser defense puzzle game that challenges your spatial reasoning, strategic planning, and quick thinking. Your mission: protect the central core from incoming laser threats by strategically positioning reflective mirrors.

Each round presents you with a set of mirrors with varying shapes and sizes. Position them wisely to deflect all incoming lasers away from the vulnerable core. The longer you survive, the higher your score!

Game Modes

Main Game Mode

The main game mode with a unique twist:

  • Mirrors are generated with a predetermined total surface area (84 units)
  • Mirror count varies per configuration, but total surface area is always uniform
  • Each new configuration is randomly generated but always fair - every player gets the same total reflective power
  • The challenge: optimal placement with limited resources
  • Infinite retries - perfect for practicing and improving your skills
Surface Area = Total reflective power available
Example: 2 large squares (40 units) + 1 trapezoid (4 units) = 84 units
All configurations total exactly 84 units for fair scoring

Daily Challenge

A special mode for competitive players:

  • One puzzle per day - everyone worldwide gets the same configuration
  • Same mirrors, same spawner positions for all players
  • Compare your survival time with players globally
  • One attempt only - make it count!
  • New challenge unlocks every 24 hours

Why Predetermined Surface Area?

The predetermined surface area system ensures fairness and strategic depth:

  • Fair Scoring: Every configuration has exactly 84 units of total surface area, so your score reflects placement skill, not luck with mirror generation
  • Strategic Trade-offs: Fewer large mirrors vs. many small mirrors - each configuration requires different strategies
  • Consistent Difficulty: No "impossible" or "trivial" rounds - every game is winnable with smart placement
  • Replayability: Infinite variety within a balanced constraint creates endless puzzle possibilities

This system was inspired by resource management in strategy games - you have a fixed "budget" of reflective power to spend wisely!

How to Play

Setup Phase

  • You're presented with several geometric mirrors of varying shapes and sizes
  • Drag and position each mirror anywhere - no grid snapping required
  • Mirrors cannot overlap with each other
  • Mirrors cannot enter the red forbidden zones (center circle + edge borders)

Defense Phase

  • Click "Launch Lasers" when you're ready
  • Lasers will spawn from multiple angles and move toward the center
  • Watch as your mirrors deflect the laser beams
  • The game continues until a laser hits the central core

Scoring

  • Your score is determined by survival time
  • The longer you keep the core protected, the higher your score
  • Performance ratings range from "Failed" to legendary status
